# The Fire Rises: Clockwork

**A Hearts of Iron IV Mod based on the book by Haku Yuzuki**

An Extract From Clockwork for Social Context:

> **SOCIAL CONTEXT**
>
> The year is 2025. The United Kingdom has fallen to Russia, losing World War III. They are now known as the Russian Breakaway State, a Moscow-aligned puppet whose landmass stretches from Cornwall to Norfolk. The puppet government is trying to revert to what they call “The Old Way,” which Russia is trying to prevent. People call this “The Russian Interjection”. The United States of America has effectively annexed Mexico which has become an American State – the 51st. Yorkshire has independence and so does Wales, Scotland and Cumberland. Northern Ireland was absorbed by the republic in the south. Asia is currently in a brutal land war between Tibet, Xinjiang and the Southeastern Army–who seem to pose a threat to the other three belligerents. Central America has once again reunited, creating a new language dialect called “Spanish (Central American).” The Earth is currently 15% scorched. The Animous Theory, suggesting all centrists are essentially on the same “team,” has been a sharp interpretation for modern politics. Developed by Keroppi Unagishima, it seems to affect modern governance in Norfolk. Most places look like the beach; land that has wasted away and houses are the last buildings left. A “Red Market” has built up as an underground alternative to the failed free market.
