# MASTER CREATOR PITCH: THE FIRE RISES: CLOCKWORK

## 1. Shared Hook

> The year is 2025. The United Kingdom has fallen to Russia, losing World War III. They are now known as the Russian Breakaway State, a Moscow-aligned puppet 
whose landmass stretches from Cornwall to Norfolk. The puppet government is trying to revert to what they call “the old way,” which Russia is trying to prevent. 
People call this “The Russian interjection”. The Earth is currently 15% scorched. The Animous Theory, suggesting all centrists are essentially on the same “team,” 
has been a sharp interpretation for modern politics. Developed by Keroppi Unagishima, it seems to affect modern governance in Norfolk. Asia is currently in a brutal land war 
between Tibet, Xinjiang and the Southeastern Army–who seem to pose a threat to the other three belligerents.

## 2. MECHANICAL CORE
* **The Animous Theory:** A new political theory where centrists are acclaimed to be closer than they are to extreme ideologies.
* **The Red Market:** A custom underground economy established to replace the failed Free Market during World War III.
* **The 51st State:** A superpower USA with Mexico being absorbed as it's fifty-first state.
